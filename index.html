About Me

Where Operations Meets Learning

My career spans two powerful worlds — Retail Operations and Learning & Development — and the intersection of both is where I create the most impact. Having managed 32+ hyperstores and over 1,300 people on the ground, I bring an operator's lens to every training program I design.

At Vishal Mega Mart, I lead L&D for a geographically distributed workforce across India. My focus is on making learning practical, scalable, and measurable — not just engaging on screen. The Store Manager Certification Program (SMCP) I designed has certified 20,000+ employees across the nation.

I actively leverage AI tools — ChatGPT, Claude, NotebookLM, and Canva AI — to build faster, more relevant training content. Automation, dashboards, and digital ecosystems are core to how I work. If it's repetitive, I find a smarter way.

"People often see Learning & Development as a support function. I see it as a business multiplier."
