About Me

Where Operations Meets Learning

I didn't start in a training room. I started on the FMCG front lines - product management, sales, distributors, targets. Then spent years running large-format retail stores across India. That ground-level journey across two demanding industries is what makes everything I build in L&D today - real, relevant, and results-focused.

As AGM - Learning & Development at Vishal Mega Mart, I lead training for one of India's largest value retail workforces. My focus is simple: make learning practical, scalable, and measurable - not just engaging on a screen.

My retail and FMCG background means I understand category, operations, and business pressures from the inside - not just from a trainer's perspective. That context changes everything about how I design learning.

I actively use AI tools - ChatGPT, Claude, NotebookLM, and Canva AI - to build faster, more relevant training content. Automation, dashboards, and digital ecosystems are core to how I work.

"The best training doesn't feel like training - it feels like preparation for something real. That's the standard I hold every program to."
